Here's a list of particular songs that have resonated throughout my life

Duran Duran

The first song that comes to mind is Ordinary World by Duran Duran, this song helped me through some really weird and difficult times, giving me hope that there is a normal life out there somewhere, just got to find it. I remember cycling a very long distance from the place I was staying at the time to my workplace along a winding busy country road with rolling hills in front of me, it was an arduous journey, but I had this song on in my headphones and it helped me put into perspective that this hardship would be temporary.

Watch the video
"There's an Ordinary World, Somehow I Have to Find!" - Duran Duran, Ordinary World

The Lotus Eaters

The song called 'First Picture of You' I first heard when I was around 5 years old. I remembered the tune as I walked around my mum's workplace, she had brought me in for the day. It was Christmas at the time, she had brought me to see the Christmas decorations. I remember a nativity scene. This music was on the radio in the background. I couldn't find the song until this year! 31 years later I found the tune had a name - First Picture of You.

Watch the video
"First Picture of You, First Picture of Summer" - The Lotus Eaters, 1981

Don Henley

This song was another crutch for me during a hard spell. The first time I heard it was DJ Sammy's dance version, which I used to listen to on long bicycle journeys. I then stumbled across Don Henley's version as I had a big 80's music rediscovery when I was at college. It helped me to power on when I was left to fend for myself in a hard time.

Watch the Don Henley version video
"I thought I knew what love was, what did I know. Those days are gone forever, I should just let them go but.." - The Boys of Summer
Watch the DJ Sammy version video

There are many more songs I go back to over the years but these are my top 3.
